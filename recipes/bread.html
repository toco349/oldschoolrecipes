Bread

Nice crispy bread.

Ingredients

  • Pot
  • Bucket
  • Grain

Steps

  1. Grind your grain into flour by using it on a windmill with a pot
  2. Fill your bucket with water at a water source
  3. Combine the pot of flour with your bucket of water
  4. Use your bread dough on any range.
  5. Enjoy your Bread!
