Pineapple Pizza

A tropicana pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pot of flour
  • Bucket of water
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Pineapple ring

Steps

  1. Combine your pot of flour and bucket of water together to create your pizza base
  2. Smear the tomato onto the pizza base to create an incomplete pizza
  3. Sprinkle your cheese onto the incomplete pizza to create an uncooked pizza
  4. Cook your uncooked pizza on a range to turn it into a plain pizza
  5. Add toppings as you wish, for this one we will be adding pineapple rings to create a pineapple pizza
  6. Enjoy your pineapple pizza!
