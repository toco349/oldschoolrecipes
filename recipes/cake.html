Cake

A plain sponge cake.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Bucket of milk
  • Pot of flour
  • Cake tin

Steps

  1. Combine all of your ingredients together with a cake tin in your inventory
  2. Use your uncooked cake on a range (you must have atleast level 40 cooking)
  3. Optional: If you have 50 cooking you can use a chocolate bar on the cake to make chocolate cake
  4. Enjoy your cake!
